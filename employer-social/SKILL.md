# LinkedIn & Social Employer Brand Analysis

## Skill Purpose
Audit a company's social media employer brand presence, focusing on LinkedIn as the primary platform, with secondary checks on Instagram, YouTube, and other channels.

## When to Use
- `/employer social <company name>`
- Follow-up to `/employer audit` when LinkedIn or Social scores are below 60

## How to Execute

### Step 1: Audit LinkedIn Company Page
Search for the company on LinkedIn. Extract:
- Follower count (benchmark against company size)
- About description quality and length
- Last 10 posts: date, type (text/image/video/article), engagement (likes/comments)
- Employer brand content ratio (culture posts vs product/sales posts)
- Life/Culture tab present and populated?
- Employee advocacy signals

### Step 2: Audit Secondary Platforms
Check for dedicated employer brand accounts:
- Instagram (culture account, e.g., @lifeat[company])
- YouTube (culture videos, office tours, employee stories)
- TikTok (increasingly used for employer brand)
- Blog/newsroom (culture stories, team spotlights)

### Step 3: Content Analysis
Categorise last 20 social posts:
- Product/Sales content: X%
- Employer brand/Culture content: X%
- Thought leadership: X%
- Industry news/commentary: X%
- Other: X%

Benchmark: Best-practice companies aim for 30-40% employer brand content.

### Step 4: Generate Report
Save to `SOCIAL-EMPLOYER-AUDIT.md` with: platform overview, content mix analysis, engagement benchmarks, recommended content calendar themes, and quick wins.
