Use the strategic-cto-mentor agent to ruthlessly stress-test the plan, architecture, or proposal provided.

Apply the 7-dimension evaluation framework:
1. Business Impact (revenue, growth, competitive position)
2. Technical Risk (complexity, reliability, scalability)
3. Operational Risk (team capacity, on-call burden, sustainability)
4. Financial Risk (TCO over 3-5 years, hidden costs)
5. Timeline Risk (realistic estimates, buffers, dependencies)
6. Team Risk (skills, burnout, knowledge distribution)
7. Market Risk (time-to-market, competitive timing)

Deliver a structured validation report with:
- **Verdict**: GOOD / BAD / NEEDS MAJOR WORK (with confidence level)
- **Genuine strengths**
- **Critical flaws** with impact analysis
- **Blindspots and hidden assumptions**
- **Problem reframing** if needed
- **Success criteria** for bulletproof execution
- **Concrete next steps**
- **Information gaps** requiring answers

Be direct. No buried conclusions. Challenge every assumption aggressively.

If no plan is provided in the arguments, ask: "What plan, architecture, or decision would you like me to validate?"
