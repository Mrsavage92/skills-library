# Privacy Policy Analysis

## Skill Purpose
Detailed review of a website's privacy policy against the 13 Australian Privacy Principles and general best practices. Identifies gaps and provides specific recommendations.

## When to Use
- `privacy policy <url>`
- Follow-up to `privacy audit` when Privacy Policy score is below 60

## How to Execute

### Step 1: Fetch and Parse Policy
Fetch the privacy policy page. Parse into sections.

### Step 2: Check Against Requirements
Score against 12 required elements:
1. Identity of data controller
2. Types of data collected
3. Purpose of collection
4. Legal basis for processing
5. Data retention periods
6. Third-party sharing details
7. Data subject rights
8. Contact information for privacy queries
9. Opt-out and deletion procedures
10. Cookie and tracking usage
11. Last updated date
12. Children's data handling

### Step 3: Readability Assessment
- Flesch-Kincaid reading level estimate
- Average sentence length
- Use of plain language vs legal jargon
- Logical structure and navigation

### Step 4: Generate Report
Save to `POLICY-ANALYSIS.md` with element checklist, readability assessment, specific gaps, recommended additions, and template sections for missing elements.
