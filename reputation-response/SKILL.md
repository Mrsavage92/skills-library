# Review Response Strategy

## Skill Purpose
Build a complete review response strategy with templates, escalation procedures, and tone guidelines. Produces a RESPONSE-STRATEGY.md that any team member can follow.

## When to Use
- `reputation response <business name>`
- Follow-up to `reputation audit` when Response Management score is below 60

## How to Execute

### Step 1: Current Response Audit
Check how the business currently responds (if at all) across all platforms. Identify patterns, tone issues, and gaps.

### Step 2: Build Response Templates
Generate customised templates for:
- **5-star reviews:** Thank personally, reference specifics, reinforce what they loved
- **4-star reviews:** Thank, acknowledge what worked, gently ask what could improve
- **3-star reviews:** Empathise, acknowledge gaps, describe what you're doing about it
- **Negative reviews (1-2 stars):** Empathise first, never get defensive, take it offline
- **Fake/spam reviews:** Flag for removal, brief professional response
- **Service recovery:** Follow-up template for after resolving an issue

### Step 3: Tone Guide
Define the brand's review response voice: warm, professional, direct, empathetic.

### Step 4: Generate Report
Save to `RESPONSE-STRATEGY.md` with templates, tone guide, escalation process, response time targets, and platform-by-platform instructions.
