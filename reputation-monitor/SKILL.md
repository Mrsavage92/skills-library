# Reputation Monitoring Setup

## Skill Purpose
Guide the business in setting up automated reputation monitoring across all platforms. Produces a MONITORING-PLAN.md with tool recommendations, alert configurations, and reporting cadence.

## When to Use
- `reputation monitor <business name>`
- Follow-up to `reputation audit` for ongoing management setup

## How to Execute

### Step 1: Platform Inventory
List every platform where the business has reviews or should have a presence.

### Step 2: Tool Recommendations
Recommend monitoring tools based on business size:
- **Free:** Google Alerts, Google Business Profile app notifications, manual weekly checks
- **Budget ($20-50/month):** ReviewTrackers, Podium basic, BirdEye starter
- **Professional ($50-200/month):** Reputation.com, Podium pro, BirdEye standard
- **Enterprise ($200+/month):** Sprinklr, Reputation.com enterprise

### Step 3: Alert Setup Guide
For each tool, provide step-by-step setup instructions for:
- New review alerts (all platforms)
- Negative review escalation (immediate notification for 1-2 star reviews)
- Weekly reputation summary
- Monthly competitor comparison

### Step 4: Generate Report
Save to `MONITORING-PLAN.md` with recommended tool stack, setup guides, alert configurations, review response SLAs, and monthly reporting template.
