# Employee Value Proposition Analysis

## Skill Purpose
Analyse and evaluate a company's EVP - the set of attributes that make them unique as an employer. Assess clarity, consistency, differentiation, and authenticity across all public touchpoints.

## When to Use
- `/employer evp <company name>`
- Follow-up to `/employer audit` when EVP score is below 60

## How to Execute

### Step 1: Extract EVP Claims
Gather every employer brand message from:
- Careers page headline and body copy
- Job posting "About Us" sections (sample 3-5 postings)
- LinkedIn company "About" description
- Glassdoor "Overview" section
- Any employer brand videos or blogs

### Step 2: Identify EVP Pillars
From the gathered messages, identify the 3-5 recurring themes. Common pillars include:
- Compensation & Benefits
- Career Growth & Learning
- Culture & Community
- Purpose & Impact
- Flexibility & Work-Life Balance
- Innovation & Technology
- Leadership & Trust

### Step 3: Test for Quality
For each claimed pillar, assess:
- **Clarity:** Can you explain it in one sentence?
- **Specificity:** Is it backed by concrete examples or just generic claims?
- **Differentiation:** Would a competitor say the exact same thing?
- **Authenticity:** Does it match what employees say in reviews?
- **Consistency:** Is it the same across all touchpoints?

### Step 4: Generate Report
Save to `EVP-ANALYSIS.md` with: identified pillars, consistency matrix, authenticity gap analysis (claims vs reviews), competitor EVP comparison, and recommended EVP framework.
